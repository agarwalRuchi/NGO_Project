Our Mission

Every specie no matter how big or small deserves to live safely. We at Safe Heavens intend to do just that. Our goal is to protect endangered species and their habitats through awareness, support, and training. We are committed to creating an enduring paradigm to conserve India's wildlife by working with local communities and government agencies to curb poaching and the illegal wildlife trade across the country.

Nationally, the animal count and species count is decreasing drastically and it is now becoming crucial to conserve our wildlife. We understand the fact that this gap can be fixed only as a community and therefore Safe Heavens is a non-profit organization that aims to re-establish the ecological balance.

In its mission to take forward the agenda of environmental protection, Safe Heaven India works with varied groups of individuals and institutions across different sections of society. These alliances strive to address the common goal of conserving biodiversity, sustainably using natural resources and maintaining ecosystems and ecosystem services for the survival of wildlife and people depending on them.

Safe Heaven primarily focusses on three aspects of this problem:
  • Curbing all illegal human activities leading to depletion of wildlife
  • Ensuring endangered species are given special protection in-order to prevent extinction
  • Ensuring that all stray animals are well fed and giving them good shelter

Our noticeable Work

Safe Heven has been striving hard to restore animal count nationally. We conduct several events throughout the year. Volunteers and members of the Safe Heaven organisation go on weekly city tours where stray animals and rescued and brought back to our rehabitalation centre. Stray animals are provided with food and put uo for adoption.
Apart from feeding the local stray animals volunteers are taken into the wild every two months where steps are taken to helpthe sanctuaries in any way under the directin of the authoritied there.
Rescuing Parrots From Fake Fortune Tellers

Freeing of parrots from the clutches of the so-called fake “Astrologers” or “Fortune Tellers” has been one of our most dynamic achievements intiated after looking at the immense cruelties being harped on these helpless parrots

Initiated on 12th December,2009

Olive Ridley Sea Turtle Conservation

We have protected 9243 nests and 9,41,806 hatchlings were successfully released in the ocean, last year itself we protected 811 nests in our coastline. On 31st October,a community-based awareness and education program in the surrounding fishing villages was implemented

31st October,2019

Stress Relief during the pandemic

The pandemic was hard not only for human beings but also for our friendly stray animals as people were scared to come out and feed strays during the pandemic. Safe Heavens set up a weekly drive where volunteers went around feeding stray animals .

Initiated on 29th March,2020

Cattle Respect Programme

Rescuing cattle like a cow, buffaloes remain our largest effort with a specialized team with specialized materials and equipment including the vehicle and non the less to mention the facilities to look after them.

20th July,2018

Tiger Protection

Excursion to Bandipur Tiger Reserve and help the local authorities with their daily activities. Volunteers spent the whole week at the reserve and helped the local authorities with any activity concerned with tiger preservation

8th January,2019
